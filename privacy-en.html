📍 MisPlace Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

1. Data Collection

MisPlace collects the following information:

  • Email address and password (for account creation)
  • Name and location of registered objects
  • GPS position when registering an object
  • Voice recordings (processed locally, not stored)

2. Use of Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Help you find your objects
  • Display your objects on the map
  • Share your objects with your family (if family subscription)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers (Europe — Zurich, Switzerland).

4. Data Sharing

Your data is never sold to third parties. It is only shared with:

  • Google Firebase (storage)
  • Anthropic Claude (anonymized voice analysis)

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time from the app or by contacting us.

6. Contact

For any questions: misplacecompany@gmail.com
